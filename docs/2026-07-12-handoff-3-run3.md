## Decision-changing findings

1. **The v0.7.0 decompose budget gate was not like-for-like** (train_base full-frame PSNR vs
   decompose foreground-masked PSNR) AND wrote the `decompose.ply` *before* its gates (a gate-failed
   solve was consumable by a manual export). Both fixed in v0.8.0 (`held_out_psnr` full-frame gate;
   `finalize_decompose` writes only after all gates; `read_verified_baseline_psnr` refuses on a
   train_base.ply-vs-metrics count mismatch). Detail: `docs/validation-m2b-phaseD-2026-07-12.md`.
2. **pxl_144634's `train_base.ply` was clobbered** with a 48k init-only model (metrics still claimed
   2.39M), faking a budget failure. Caught by the panel, regenerated, re-run clean. **Root cause
   unknown** — the known writers (perf/smoke) target scratch dirs, not `assets/built/`. Evidence
   preserved in scratchpad. The new baseline consistency check guards the class at decompose time.
3. **The decompose-recovered foliage normals are near-isotropic** (‖mean-normal‖≈0.20). Relighting
   still responds to *meaningful* light moves (overhead vs grazing) — the orbit video's elevation
   sweep exploits exactly this and reads as responsive (relit std 0.029) — but *small* light-arc
   moves barely register, and the **appearance-PSNR budget gate does NOT catch normal quality**.
   Seeded as **DECISIONS D5** (needs the owner eyeball on the orbit video to close/keep).
