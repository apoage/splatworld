## Surprises / gotchas (all fixed; recipe in decisions.md)
- COLMAP registered ALL 204 repetitive-foliage frames — the gravel-path texture anchored it.
- gsplat 1.5.3 JIT-compiles `_C` at first `rasterization()`, not at pip install — that
  surfaced the whole CUDA chain: `TORCH_CUDA_ARCH_LIST` had `10.3` (torch rejects) → pin 8.6;
  `CUDA_HOME` pointed at system toolkit → point at env; conda toolkit resolved to 13.1
  (incompatible w/ torch cu124) → pin 12.4; env gcc was 15 (CUDA 12.4 needs ≤13) → gcc 13.
- 2.39M gaussians for one asset ≫ the ≤1.5M-whole-carpet budget → `tasks/2026-07-11-perf-budget.md`.
